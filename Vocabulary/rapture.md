# rapture
rapture /ˈræptʃə(r)/ /ˈræptʃɚ/ 

## 不可数名词

狂喜；欣喜若狂

Rapture is a feeling of extreme happiness or pleasure.  

> The film was shown to gasps of rapture at the Democratic Convention... 
> 在民主党大会上放映的电影使人们十分欣喜。


> His speech was received with rapture by his supporters...
> 他的演说受到支持者们的热烈欢迎。
